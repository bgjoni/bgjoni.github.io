Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is, to give accurate information to the drivers ,and residents about the weather conditions. Drivers mostly will use the website to know the weather conditions before travelling, during winter months. Information that this site will offer to its users will be: temperature, the highest and the lowest. It will show the wind speed. We expect drivers to plan according their trip and cancel some trips in severe weather.It will provide weather conditions for residents so they get dressed accordingly for the day.

Target Audience Description

Target for this website will be, people from different age group,from college students to people in late 50- ties. Income will vary from user to user. Weather forecast is important from high school students to director of big company.Mainly will be used by drivers and residents of this area. It be for the residents as well for the drivers from this area or people driving though this area. It will be mobile friendly since many people travel and are outside.Wheather site will help people to decide whether to walk to the train station or take a taxi;deepending on weather conditions.

How the Site Will Meet the Needs of the Users?

This is weather site and I ask myself this question: what do users need from weather site? The residents need to know, how is going to be the weather tomorrow and coming week.Is going to snow or not,is going to be winy or not. The drivers would want to know the dangerous routes so they can avoid them. Drivers will need to know blocked routes. This site will help chose their route according to the weather.The site will collect data from the police patrolling the roads. Different age groups expected to visit the website so it will be easy to navigate,through simple design.It will have little information but important one. Most of the users expected to use mobile devices so it will support very well this technology.
