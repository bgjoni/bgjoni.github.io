Visitor Analysis and Persona Good Experience

Emirates

URL: http://www.emirates.com/us/english/

Visitor Analysis

Emirates airline, is airline company that flies in many countries around the world.This website has wide audience. People over twentis years probably will visit this website.These people have average or higher income, because company is not very cheap. These people want to fly with good airline. They live somewhere where they have internet connection and emirates airlines flies in those countries. This website provides some important information for someone who wants to fly; such as book a flight check flight status, manage booking. These buttons are located on the right side. They are in red background that attract the user attention. These are main services that visitors are looking for. It is easy to navigate even if you do not have experience. There is beautiful picture of a lady and little boy enjoying flying. It is pleasant experience.

Persona: Jeff Herr

Age: 45

Work Experience: Over 25 Years

Jeff is an American living and working in Dubai. He travels often to America to visit his family. He visits this site often to find good deals, on flights to US. He normally visits this site while he is at work. He is having his lunch and is checking for some flights. He wants to spend Christmas with his family in New York. Emirates is very good airline, has very comfortable aircraft. He wants to enjoy his long flight.

He uses his laptop in his office when he visits the site. He takes the metro on the way home and check this sites again on his smartphone. He normally spends 20 minutes in a week. He chooses this website because it is easy to navigate, secure and fulfill his needs as flight passenger.
